## Identified Bottlenecks & Manual Handoff Points

| # | Process Step | Issue | Impact | Source |
|---|-------------|-------|--------|--------|
| 1 | Conflict check → Salesforce | Manual data entry; no prod integration | Onboarding delays; data errors | March 24 session |
| 2 | Matter party entry | Inconsistent format; text field not structured table | Cannot reliably parse or automate | March 24 session |
| 3 | Salesforce → STAR | Works via DbAmp but dependent on NCA approval | Bottleneck at approval stage | March 24 session |
| 4 | Client data across 5+ systems | No single source of truth for industry classification | Conflicting data for marketing, BD, service lines | March 30 session |
| 5 | Network drive organization | Manually created folder structures, no naming standards | Documents not discoverable for AI | March 30 session |
| 6 | CaseWare data access | Locked inside Citrix app; no API or export path defined | Financial statements inaccessible to AI pipeline | March 30 session |
| 7 | Records retention enforcement | Manual, aspirational; no measurement across firm | Compliance risk; data beyond retention periods | March 30 session |
| 8 | AI initiative coordination | Multiple teams building similar tools independently | Wasted effort; fragmented solutions | March 30 session |
